Welcome to the Unit Planner User Guide

This user guide will help you make the most out of our Unit Planner website. Here, we'll explain how to use the website effectively to plan your academic journey.

Getting Started

When you visit our Unit Planner, you'll see a list of available units. Each unit is represented by a box with colors indicating its status:

  • - This indicates a completed unit.
  • - This indicates an incomplete unit.

You can change the status of a unit by clicking on the "x" mark inside the box. Toggle between completed and incomplete as needed.

Prerequisites and Post-requisites

Some units have prerequisites, which are units that you must complete before taking the current unit. These prerequisites are highlighted in blue.

Post-requisites are units that require the current unit as a prerequisite. These are highlighted in orange.

Understanding prerequisites and post-requisites is essential for planning your academic path effectively.

Optimizing Your Plan

Our Unit Planner helps you optimize your study plan to graduate in the quickest time possible. By marking completed and incomplete units and understanding prerequisites, you can efficiently plan your academic journey.
